One of the goals for the Women Writers Vector Toolkit is to increase classroom access to conducting vector analyses on early women’s texts. We offer other tools and materials to help teachers use the uniquely digital dimensions of WWVT more effectively in their teaching. In the teaching exploration sections, you’ll find a searchable database of assignments created for the WWVT and suggested searches to show students different results when using word vectors. For more reading resources and potential texts to assign about word vector analysis, check out these helpful sources.
