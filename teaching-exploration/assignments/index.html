One of WWVT project’s goals is to make available potential assignments that could be created based around the application as well as the resources provided. The assignments below have been written for different levels of familiarity with the corpus, the vectors tool, and word vector modeling more generally. The assignments are also a variety of classroom-based activities and take-home assignments.

If you have created your own assignments, lesson plans, or curriculum based on the WWVT and would like to publish it, email us at wwp@northeastern.edu.

Introduction to Word Vector Analysis

The classroom-based activity can serve as a starting point for incorporating Women Writers Online, word vectors, and text analysis in the classroom.
