Navigating the Women Writers Vector Toolkit



We imagine this space to be one to foster curiosity rather than steer the user in the direction of what should be researched. We encourage you to use the Word Vector Interface or even follow our documentation process to conduct a word vector analysis on another TEI/XML encoded corpus of your own! Because this project is built upon accessibility and transparency for users with myriad levels of text analysis experience, we have structured this site to be used to:

  • share the Word Vector Interface for researchers, instructors, and learners to explore.
  • show the process of regularizing the data by publishing the methodology used by team members;
  • suggest teaching implementations by providing assignments and suggested searches (to be available soon);
  • provide a resources section with an annotated readings list and glossary;
  • publish case studies (to be available soon) that demonstrate how to analyze the different corpus models using the word vectors tool;
